Name of QuantLet : SFEarch1mc

Published in: Statistics of Financial Markets

Description: ’Monte Carlo Simulation of ARCH(1)-process with 1000 repetitions. Estimates alpha-parameter with Quasi Maximum Likelihood method. Shows summary statistics for different sample sizes: 1) mean of all estimates; 2) average deviation from true alpha; 3) percentage of parameters estimated equal or lager than one.
Default parameter settings: alpha = 0, omega = 0.2’

Keywords: monte-carlo, simulation, garch, stock-price, time-series


Author: Sophie Burgard
